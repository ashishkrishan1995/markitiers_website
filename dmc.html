Delhi Marketing Conclave

by Markitiers, 13 April 2017

Delhi Marketing Conclave

First of its kind Marketing Conference in Delhi bringing together the masterminds of this amazing business of selling and promoting! An initiative by Markitiers equally in association with https://www.facebook.com/kmc.fic/ equally committed to the market players as well as the new-buds in the industry.
AIM: To decode the field of Marketing to aspirants through a comprehensive summit that changes the mind-set of the participants and to ignite the spirit of Marketing among the youth in India.
MOTIVE: DMS seeks to achieve this by means of various competitions and events that not only test the participant’s ability to ‘Market’ but also, at the same time, are fun and open the participants to new avenues by allowing them to access skills they never knew existed in them.
VISION: The Delhi Marketing summit targets to provide the perfect blend of fun and knowledge so as to introduce participants to the exhilarating, liberating, and creative field which is marketing.


The list of contests and events which are supposed to happen in DMS are indexed below:
• Opening Ceremony: A detailed introduction to the college students about our junctures. The event will be kick started after being inaugurated by our honorable guest of honors for the ceremony. In their eminent presence, we’ll be discussing the rapidly growing trends in this Marketing Industry most important of them being Youth Marketing and how is it taking over the business.

• Panel Discussion: With the amazing group of marketers adorning our panel we’ll be having an informative and in-depth discussion on the present scenarios dealing with our topic-“Why is digital marketing given an edge over offline marketing?”

• Brand Catechize: A guess-the-brand-challenge in which we will be announcing famous taglines, slogans, founders, inventors and logos of the brands where the students ought to recognize the correct brand name.

• Market Expedition: A marketing treasure hunt, “Dhoondho aur Becho”. Participants will have to hunt for their products and create a marketing strategy to sell their respective products.

• M-plan (college): The Million Dollar M-Plan: Quest of the Marketing Genius- A case study would be provided to the students and they’ll have to prepare a case solution, stating their marketing strategy regarding the same. The top 10 participants will be shortlisted who’ll be pitching their ideas on the final day of the event to prove their mettle in formulating marketing idea.

•The M-plan contest for the startups, We Market: the contest will be encompassing a wide range of people in the industry- ranging from well-established startups, well known players holding years of experience, struggling startups who have stepped into the market but are striving hard to make it bug and last but not the least our budding entrepreneurs who have great ideas hovering in their impressionable minds! Participants will be asked to pitch in the marketing strategies and the business plans they are employing in their startups to achieve something big out of their idea.

• By the end of the event day, we will be highlighting the delightful moments and people of the event plus the winners of the contests will be awarded.
